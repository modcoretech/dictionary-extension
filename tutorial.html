Let's Learn About Your Dictionary!

Finding Word Meanings

See a word you don't know? Let's find out what it means!

  1. Type and Search: Put the word in the box at the top and click "Search" or press "Enter".
  2. Right-Click Lookup: See a word on a webpage? Select it, right-click, and pick "Look Up Word".

Making Your Search Special (Filters)

Want to see only nouns or verbs? Click "Part of Speech" to open the filter options and check the boxes.

Hearing Words Speak

Want to hear how a word sounds? Click the "Listen" button after searching.

Making It Yours (Settings)

Change how it looks! Click the extension icon and pick "Options". Change colors, voices, fonts, and more!

Cleaning Old Words (Clear Cache)

If it shows old words, clean them up! Go to "Options" and click "Clear Cache".

Quick Looks (Popup)

Select a word, right click, and pick 'Look up word'. A little window shows the meaning.
